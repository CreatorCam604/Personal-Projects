Package Details

What's included?

All packages include accommodation, specified meals, activities, and ground transfers as listed. International flights are not included but we can assist with booking.

Cancellation policy

Full refund up to 30 days before departure. 50% refund between 15-29 days. No refund within 14 days. We recommend travel insurance for all bookings.

Group discounts

Travelling with 4 or more? Groups of 4-7 receive 10% off. Groups of 8+ receive 15% off. Contact us for corporate or event pricing.
